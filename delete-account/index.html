Delete Account

You can delete your Ramble account in two ways.

When you delete your account, we delete your account login information and your list of friends. There is no way to reactivate your account after deletion.

From the App

You can delete your account from the profile page of the Ramble iOS or Android app.

By Email

You can also email support@ramble.co with "account deletion" in the subject line and ask us to delete your account.
