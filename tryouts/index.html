Ramble Tryouts

Three landing-page experiments for Ramble. Each option keeps the ramblephant front-and-center, but plays with a different era.

Minimalist

Quiet, clean, modern

Spacious, editorial, and stripped down to the essentials. Calm and minimal.

1999

Geocities nostalgia

Tables, tiled backgrounds, and a bit of glitter. Feels like a dial-up time capsule.

2005

Glossy era

A glossy 2005 style with gradients, soft shadows, and chunky buttons.

2015

Flat and friendly

Bold blues, flat cards, and clean UI blocks. Feels like a 2015 app splash page.

Contemporary

Soft glass, big type

Airy gradients, glassy panels, and modern typography with a calm palette.

1970s

Groovy analog web

Warm tones, chunky borders, and retro curves. A 70s alternate-universe website.

Apple

Apple product vibe

Clean, glossy, and minimal with generous spacing and subtle shadows.

Classic Apple

Skeuomorphic era

Brushed metal, aqua buttons, and early 2000s Apple polish.

90s Apple

Apple Garamond era

Clean white layout, quiet rules, and serif typography inspired by 90s Apple branding.

Time Machine

UI chooser concepts

Four different UI controls for letting people pick a time travel era.
