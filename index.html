Ramble is the easiest way to have spontaneous phone calls with your friends.

  1. Download the app and invite some friends.
  2. When you’re ready to chat, put on your sneakers* and start a Ramble.
  3. Whoever's free joins for a chat.

Ever thought you could use your phone to make actual phone calls? Crazy!
